Privacy by Design

Last Updated: February 2026

TL;DR

Atmos Agent does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

1 No Data Collection

Atmos Agent operates entirely locally in your browser. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Send data to external servers

2 Local Storage Only

The Script may use your browser's local storage for:

  • Your preference settings
  • Temporary UI state

This data never leaves your computer.

3 No External Requests

The Script makes no external network requests except:

  • Loading Feather Icons from unpkg.com
  • Fetching configuration (rules, version) from GitHub Pages

4 Platform Data

The Script accesses page data (CallTools or Gmail) only to provide its features. This data is:

  • Processed locally in your browser
  • Never transmitted elsewhere
This privacy policy reflects our commitment to your privacy. We believe software should respect users.
