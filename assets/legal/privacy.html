Privacy by Design

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

TL;DR

CallTools Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

1 No Data Collection

CallTools Pro operates entirely locally in your browser. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use cookies or analytics

2 Local Storage Only

The Script may use your browser's local storage for:

  • Your preference settings (e.g., search format toggle)
  • Temporary UI state (e.g., modal visibility)

This data never leaves your computer and can be cleared by clearing your browser data.

3 No External Requests

The Script makes no external network requests except:

  • Loading Feather Icons from unpkg.com CDN
  • Checking for updates from GitHub Pages (version check only)

4 CallTools Platform Data

The Script accesses CallTools.io page data only to provide its features. This data is:

  • Processed locally in your browser
  • Never transmitted elsewhere
  • Used only for displaying compliance info and timezone data

5 Your Rights

Since we collect no data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. You can:

  • Uninstall the Script anytime
  • Clear browser local storage
  • Disable auto-updates in Tampermonkey
This privacy policy reflects our commitment to your privacy. We believe software should respect users.
